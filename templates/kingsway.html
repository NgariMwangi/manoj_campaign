Dr Manoj Shah is The Group Managing Director of The Kingsway Group of Companies, which has a Family Legacy of over 65 Years. He has been a Pioneer and Involved in The Growth of the Group Business for Over 46 Years. The Group is Very Dynamic and Evolving at a Rapid Pace over the Last 2 Decades in The Hospitality, Automobile, Energy, and Property Development Sectors in The East African Region.

The companies are spread out from the hospitality industry to the financial sector where excellence has become an attitude. An attitude that sees them performing highly by coming up with new concepts and breaking new ground in the industries of East and Central Africa.

The Following is a Brief Profile of The Groups Leading Businesses.

  1. KINGSWAY TYRES LIMITED

    The Anchor and Traditional Family Business that has Been the Backbone and Pillar of Success for The Groups Phenomenal Growth. Over 25 Retail Outlets all over The Country and Supported by a 100,000 Sq Feet Central Warehouse and Robust Logistic. The State- of-The-Art Tyre Centers Provide Essential Services to all Motor Users with The Latest State of Art Equipment.

    Kingsway Tyres Ltd are the sub distribution of the biggest and most assured names, Tyres have to offer Michelin, BF Goodrich, Apollo, Kumho, Goodyear and Tecking. These are respected international brands that call home all over Kenya.

    Kingsway Tyres Ltd has set up a state of art retreading factory with a capacity of producing 20000 Tyres annually.

  2. THE VILLAGE MARKET

    A state-of-the-art shopping and recreational complex is situated at the heart of one of Nairobi's fastest growing residential suburbs, Gigiri. The complex adapted the concept of an open-air Market environment (which traditionally exists in every African village) to a shopping mall setting.

    Visitors and holidaymakers alike shop and relax in this environment that boasts of international standards.

  3. THE TRIBE HOTEL: -THE TRIBE IS A PHILOSOPHY

    Tribe Hotel welcomes guests to a luxurious experience. The hotel maintains a benchmark of excellence in every aspect of its offerings. The 128 guestrooms and suites excite and soothe at the same time - rich deep colors mix with natural stone and wood, stainless steel and glass features blend with rustic metal and handmade wooden furniture, world- class amenities and new age technology seamlessly complement local fabrics and artifacts.

    The new hotel at The Village Market was conceived from a simple belief: That there is only One Planet and there is only One Tribe that matters...humankind. From the hotel's Inspired architecture and interiors to the staff's singular focus on exemplary service, a common vision pervades: the unity of form and function celebrated within the diversity of cultures, styles and materials.
