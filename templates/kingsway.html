Dr Manoj Shah is The Group Managing Director of The Kingsway Group of Companies, which has a Family Legacy of over 65 Years. He has been a Pioneer and involved in the growth of the Group business for Over 46 Years. The Group is very dynamic and evolving at a rapid pace over the last 2 decades in The Hospitality, Automobile, Energy, and Property Development Sectors in The East African Region.
