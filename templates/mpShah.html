Every sunrise is an invitation for us to arise and brighten someone's day. Manoj has a great attraction to work for the reduction of miseries of people, and the place of worship to do that is a hospital. He is looking after the 2 hospitals with immense involvement and care. Though the outcome looks bright and beautiful from the outside, at times it isn't the same in the core working from inside. If you want to believe in your purpose, you have to keep company with people who believe in your purpose too.

The biggest challenge that Manoj occasionally faces in the hospitals that he manages is getting the Board and Management on the same page and working together as a team. There are many times a difference in opinions. But the inspired and visionary Manoj opines that the whole objective is to see the bigger picture and what is best for the hospital. Mahatma Gandhi succinctly quoted, "A small group of determined and like-minded people can change the course of history." Manoj's intuition is very sharp. He always finds building relations with righteous people more productive than battling with egos and small minds. Working with like minded people has always been a pleasure for Manoj, as he wants to relate only with people who can help him advance in his life purpose. However he experiences a lot of pain at work with people who are not on the same page, as he wants to stay away from those who hinder his path, even if it is at their unconscious level. In Lionism, he has always found politics and ambition before self, the biggest killers of all the social efforts as leaders. Though he feels the onus is on the leaders only to strive to reconcile and sort out differences, many a time this has been a big challenge.
